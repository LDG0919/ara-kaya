Data Deletion Request

App: Abak Futures Calc - PnL, Liq
Developer: Ara-Kaya
Last updated: April 26, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of any data related to your use of the Abak Futures Calc - PnL, Liq app.

What Data Does the App Store?

Abak Futures Calc does not maintain any user accounts or store user data on our servers. The App is a calculator tool that operates locally on your device. The following types of data may exist:

  • On your device (local only): App settings, calculation history, and user preferences
  • Held by third parties (Google): Advertising identifiers and ad-related data collected by Google AdMob; aggregated usage statistics collected by Google Play Services

How to Delete Your Data

Step 1 — Delete Local Data

Uninstall the Abak Futures Calc app from your device. All locally stored data (settings, calculation history, preferences) will be removed automatically.

  • Android: Long-press the Abak Futures Calc icon → Uninstall (or go to Settings → Apps → Abak Futures Calc → Uninstall)

Step 2 — Reset Advertising Identifier (Optional)

To prevent further ad-related data collection by Google AdMob:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (or Reset advertising ID)

Step 3 — Contact Us for Additional Requests

If you have additional concerns or want confirmation that all data related to your use of the App has been removed, please contact us:

Email: ldgu0919@naver.com

Please include the following in your email:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request - Abak Futures Calc"
  • Your device model (optional)
  • Approximate date you started using the App (optional)

We will respond to your request within 30 days. Note that since we do not maintain user accounts or server-side data, our response will typically confirm that no server-side data exists for you.

Third-Party Data

For data held by Google (advertising data, usage statistics), please refer directly to:

  • Google Account Settings
  • Google Privacy Policy
