Data Deletion Request

App: 스르륵 (Seureureuk) - Korean Multilingual Dictionary
Developer: Ara-Kaya
Last updated: April 29, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of any data related to your use of the 스르륵 (Seureureuk) - Korean Multilingual Dictionary app.

What Data Does the App Store?

스르륵 (Seureureuk) does not maintain any user accounts or store user data on our servers. The following types of data may exist:

  • On your device (local only): App settings (UI language, mode, traditional/simplified Chinese preference), bookmarks, user-added dictionary entries
  • Held by third parties (Google): Advertising identifiers and ad-related data collected by Google AdMob; in-app purchase records held by Google Play Billing

How to Delete Your Data

Step 1 — Delete Local Data

Uninstall the 스르륵 app from your device. All locally stored data (settings, bookmarks, user-added entries) will be removed automatically.

  • Android: Long-press the 스르륵 icon → Uninstall (or go to Settings → Apps → 스르륵 → Uninstall)

Step 2 — Reset Advertising Identifier (Optional)

To prevent further ad-related data collection by Google AdMob:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (or Reset advertising ID)

Step 3 — Contact Us for Additional Requests

If you have additional concerns or want confirmation that all data related to your use of the App has been removed, please contact us:

Email: ldgu0919@naver.com

Please include the following in your email:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request - 스르륵"
  • Your device model (optional)
  • Approximate date you started using the App (optional)

We will respond to your request within 30 days. Note that since we do not maintain user accounts or server-side data, our response will typically confirm that no server-side data exists for you.

Third-Party Data

For data held by Google (advertising data, purchase records), please refer directly to:

  • Google Account Settings
  • Google Privacy Policy
