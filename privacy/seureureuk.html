Privacy Policy

App: Seureureuk - Korean Multilingual Dictionary
Last updated: April 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Seureureuk - Korean Multilingual Dictionary ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information directly from you. The App is a dictionary tool that operates primarily on your device.

However, we use third-party services that may collect information automatically, including:

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, language settings
  • Advertising identifiers: Google Advertising ID (AAID) for ad personalization
  • Usage data: Aggregated and anonymized statistics about how the App is used
  • Approximate location: Country-level location derived from IP address (for ad targeting)
  • Purchase information: When you purchase the "Remove Ads" item, transaction details are processed by Google Play Billing

Locally stored data such as app settings (display language, mode, traditional/simplified Chinese preference) and user-added dictionary entries remain on your device and are not transmitted to us.

2. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob – For displaying advertisements. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Services – For app distribution and core functionality. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing – For processing in-app purchases (Remove Ads). Google Privacy Policy
  • Device Text-to-Speech (TTS) – Pronunciation playback uses your device's built-in TTS engine. No audio data is sent to us.

3. How We Use Information

The information collected by third-party services is used to:

  • Display relevant advertisements within the App
  • Measure ad performance and prevent fraud
  • Process in-app purchases and verify entitlements
  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Information collected by third-party services (such as Google AdMob and Google Play Billing) is governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Advertising preferences: You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Remove Ads: You can remove all advertisements via a one-time in-app purchase. Once purchased, no further ad-related data will be collected by the App.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland: You have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. You can manage your consent preferences within the App.

For users in California (CCPA) and other US states: You have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

6. Data Retention

We do not store personal information on our servers. Locally stored app data is removed when you uninstall the App. Data collected by third-party services is retained according to their respective policies.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. International Data Transfers

Information collected through third-party services may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States. These transfers are subject to appropriate safeguards.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
