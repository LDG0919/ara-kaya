Data Deletion Request

App: Star catcher - 9-language Korean-centered vocabulary
Developer: Ara-Kaya
Last updated: May 10, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of any data related to your use of the Star catcher app.

What Data Does the App Store?

Star catcher does not maintain any user accounts or store user data on our servers. All user data is kept on your device only. The following types of data may exist:

  • On your device (local only): Bookmarks, hide-known-words list, custom entries, quiz and learning progress, and preferences such as background image, colors, and text size
  • Files you've exported: Backup files (.json) saved to your device through the in-app backup feature
  • Held by third parties (Google): Advertising identifiers and ad-related data collected by Google AdMob; aggregated usage statistics collected by Google Play Services; in-app purchase records (remove ads) kept by your Google Play account

How to Delete Your Data

Step 1 — Delete Local Data

Uninstall the Star catcher app from your device. All locally stored data (bookmarks, custom entries, learning progress, preferences) will be removed automatically.

  • Android: Long-press the Star catcher icon → Uninstall (or go to Settings → Apps → Star catcher → Uninstall)

If you want to keep the App but only clear stored data: Settings → Apps → Star catcher → Storage → "Clear data". You can also clear bookmarks, custom entries, and learning history individually from within the App's settings.

Step 2 — Delete Backup Files (.json)

If you've exported a .json backup using the in-app backup feature, that file is stored in your device's Downloads folder or wherever you saved it. You'll need to delete it manually using your file manager. These files are saved on your own device and are never transmitted to our servers.

Step 3 — Reset Advertising Identifier (Optional)

To prevent further ad-related data collection by Google AdMob:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (or Reset advertising ID)

Step 4 — In-App Purchase (Remove Ads)

If you've made the one-time in-app purchase to remove ads, that record is kept by your Google Play account, not by us. Purchase history can be reviewed or managed through:

  • Google Play Order History

For refund or purchase deletion requests, please contact Google Play support directly.

Step 5 — Contact Us for Additional Requests

If you have additional concerns or want confirmation that all data related to your use of the App has been removed, please contact us:

Email: ldgu0919@naver.com

Please include the following in your email:

  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request - Star catcher"
  • Your device model (optional)
  • Approximate date you started using the App (optional)

We will respond to your request within 30 days. Note that since we do not maintain user accounts or server-side data, our response will typically confirm that no server-side data exists for you.

Third-Party Data

For data held by Google (advertising data, usage statistics, in-app purchase records), please refer directly to:

  • Google Account Settings
  • Google Privacy Policy
